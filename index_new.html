About



I'm Maria and i'm currently working as a 3D Artist in the digital fashion industry in the beautiful Porto (Portugal) for almost 3 years now! Gladly I can do a bit of everything from modeling, texturing to rendering and art direction! I moved here right after finishing my course - Arts and Multimedia - in my hometown Viseu in July 2017.
I can describe myself as a dreamer. My mind constantly wanders around always looking for inspiration in everything I'm emerged in, there's not the need to go too far to find inspiration I believe.
Since I studied classical art and drawing that I have a particular taste for dark art as you will see in some of my works and I'm fascinated by robots and mechanics. Pixar movie "Wall-E" was actually what made me start this journey as a 3D Artist so I guess I've always kept that strong connection with innanimated creatures coming life through animation and touching the viewer's heart.
As mainly self-taught in 3D before having the chance to study and work on it, I'm always up for a good challenge and I'm constantly researching and experimenting to expand my knowledge and keep evolving as an artist.
Oh and I love to travel solo for some new adventures and I've been in some places mainly in Europe exploring and doing volunteer work so moving to a new place easily means "challenge accepted"!
Say hi and let's talk!

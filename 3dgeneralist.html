Loop - 3D Animation

LOOP was a 3D short animation made as a graduation project. I produced and directed all of it and did everything in it with the guiding of two tutors. Below you can see some of the process, some concepts, the various stages of modeling, the creation of the scenarios, rigging and shading.

Software: Blender, Krita, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Photoshop










Experiments II

One of my daily renders to practice shading and lighting. All the post-processing of the image such as color correction and other enhancements was done in the same software.

Software: Blender



Experiments III

Another of my daily renders mostly to practice shading since I already had the 3D model of the hand done for a previous project. All the post-processing of the image was done in the same software.

Software: Blender





Experiments IV

Another of my daily renders to practice shading but also post-processing using the compositor nodes in Blender.

Software: Blender




Experiments V

Daily render to practice shading and experiment with Blender modifiers.

Software: Blender




Experiments I

Daily render to practice shading and a bit of sculpting.

Software: Blender



Airplane - Hard Surface Modeling

Based on the concept by Sergey Glushakov (http://dinodrawing.deviantart.com/) I created this 3D model of an airplane to practice hard surface modeling techniques

Software: Blender



Finis Operis - 3D & 2D Animation

Short animation mixing 2D and 3D art created in collaboration with a friend. I was responsible for the 3D modeling, shading and animation while he was responsible for the 2D animation and editing.

Software: Blender, Krita, Gimp






Presentation - 2D Motion Graphics

Short presentation of my CV made for the '2D Animation' subject of my course. It was more of an experiment to try motion graphics with a 3D software. At the end, I believe it saved me some time since I had a whole lot of different options in a 3D environment and, most important, I had the opportunity to learn more.

I created the more complex illustrations in Adobe Illustrator but most of them were created directly in Blender and animated there.

Software: Blender, Adobe Illustrator
